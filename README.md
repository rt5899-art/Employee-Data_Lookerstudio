## Employee Data Analytics Dashboard
An interactive, data-driven HR Analytics Dashboard built to monitor organizational health, workforce demographics, salary distributions, and hiring trends. This dashboard translates raw employee data into actionable strategic insights for executive decision-making.

#### Project Repository: [Employee-Data_Lookerstudio](https://datastudio.google.com/s/tAOCWEsKxpI)

### Project Overview
This project delivers a comprehensive workforce overview by analyzing 1,000 total employee records. It enables HR leaders and executives to drill down by Date Range, Department, and Age to monitor retention risks, track diversity, evaluate global footprint distribution, and optimize compensation structures.

### Tools & Technologies

•	Data Visualization: Google Looker Studio
•	Data Source Integration: CSV/Google Sheets
•	Key Features: Interactive Geo-Maps, Tree-maps, Time-Series Forecasting Trends, and Dynamic KPI Cards.

 ### Key Business Insights
•	Workforce Capacity: Currently managing 1,000 total employees, with 897 active working employees and an exit rate of 10.30%.

•	Compensation Standards: The organization commands a Median Salary of $92,806, with executive leadership roles (Vice Presidents and Directors) consuming the highest cumulative annual salary blocks.

•	Gender Diversity: Maintained a healthy gender balance with a Female-to-Male ratio of 1.06 (53.2% Female vs. 46.8% Male).

•	Departmental Distribution: Specialty Products holds the majority share with 266 employees, followed closely by Corporate (254) and Research & Development (254).

•	Hiring Momentum: Record counts have seen exponential growth over the years, escalating drastically post-2013 and peaking near 2021.

•	Geographic Footprint: Strong global talent concentration predominantly centered in North America, with emerging footprints in South America and Asia.

### Strategic Recommendations

•	Insight: A 10.30% exit rate indicates a steady turnover risk.
➔ Action: Conduct targeted stay-interviews and department-specific sentiment surveys where attrition is highest to implement localized retention strategies.

•	Insight: Exponential hiring growth trends captured post-2018 put pressure on onboarding infrastructure.

➔ Action: Automate and scale HR onboarding processes to maintain employee engagement during high-volume recruitment cycles.

•	Insight: Heavy compensation spend is highly concentrated at top executive tiers (Vice Presidents).

➔ Action: Review salary benchmarks for mid-to-junior technical roles (e.g., Network/Cloud Administrators) to ensure market competitiveness and prevent talent poaching in critical tech functions.

•	Insight: Core talent clusters reside heavily in North America.

➔ Action: Leverage the established infrastructure in secondary regions (Asia/South America) to spin up cost-effective satellite teams and access diverse global talent pools.
